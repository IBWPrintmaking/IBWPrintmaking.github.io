About Our Program

Established in 2019, the printmaking program at Ida B. Wells High School offers students a unique blend of traditional techniques and contemporary applications. Under the guidance of our dedicated art instructor, Ms. Sarah Thompson, students explore various printmaking methods including relief printing, intaglio, and screen printing.
